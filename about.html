More About Us

Besides the thrift store, the Center for International Education and Small Business Development (CIESBD) is a non-profit organization based in College Park Maryland. CIESBD was founded by Mammah Johnathan in 1994 in order to provide services and skills to needy residents necessary to improve their lives, and building community awareness for people who are mostly underprivileged.
